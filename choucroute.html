Choucroute

Description:

Choucroute is a traditional dish from Alsace (Eastern France). It consists of sauerkraut with sausages and other salted meats, charcuterie, and potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • Sauerkraut
  • Bacon
  • Onions
  • Pork fat or butter
  • Parsley and bay leaves
  • Salt and peppercorn
  • Dry white wine
  • Duck or goose meat
  • Sausages
  • Ham
  • Smoked pork loin

Steps:

  1. Drain the sauerkraut
  2. Slice the bacon in 1/2 inch cubes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees
  4. Slowly cook the bacon and onions in butter in a covered casserole for about 10 minutes, without browning. Stir in the sauerkraut, cover and cook slowly for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the herbs and spices. Pour some wine and season with salt and pepper.
  6. In a covered dish, cook slowly in the oven for 3 to 3 1/2 hours.
  7. Brown the meats in a skillet, and add them to the sauerkraut
  8. Continue to simmer in the oven for an extra 1 to 1 1/2 hours. Enjoy!
